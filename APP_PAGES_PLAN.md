## Alaca - Post-Login Page Plan

### 1. Dashboard
- **Purpose**: High-level overview and daily actions.
- **Key content**:
  - Today’s workout (card with start button).
  - Today’s calories/macros vs target.
  - Quick progress chart (weight/BMI).
  - Active challenge summary.
  - Quick stats: streak, completed workouts this week, unread notifications.

### 2. Profile
- **Purpose**: Manage personal and health information.
- **Views**:
  - **My profile (editable)**:
    - Avatar, display name, bio.
    - Height, weight, BMI, gender, birthday.
    - Goals (lose fat / gain muscle / maintain).
  - **Public profile (read-only)**:
    - Basic info and stats.
    - Recent posts.
    - Follow/Unfollow button.

### 3. Workouts
- **Workouts list**:
  - List of user-created workouts.
  - Filters: type, intensity, duration.
- **Workout detail**:
  - Exercises with sets, reps, rest, notes.
  - Estimated duration and calories.
- **Create/Edit workout**:
  - Select exercises from Exercise Library.
  - Configure sets/reps, rest, notes, intensity.
- **Workout session view**:
  - Step-through mode to run the workout.
  - Mark sets completed, mark workout finished.

### 4. Workout Schedule
- **Schedule view**:
  - Weekly calendar or list of upcoming workouts.
  - Status per item: planned, completed, skipped.
- **Actions**:
  - Schedule workout.
  - Reschedule or cancel.
  - Mark as completed (integrates with progress stats).

### 5. Nutrition
- **Food Library**:
  - Search and filter foods.
  - View macros and serving sizes.
- **Nutrition Plans**:
  - List of user plans (cutting, bulking, maintenance).
  - Detail: meals per day, items per meal, totals.
  - Create/Edit plan: set calorie target/macros, add foods.
- **Today’s Meals / Intake**:
  - Today’s logged meals.
  - Running total vs target (calories & macros).
  - Quick add food or copy from previous days.

### 6. Progress & Analytics
- **Progress timeline**:
  - Entries for weight, BMI, measurements, notes, photos.
  - CRUD for progress entries.
- **Charts & analytics**:
  - Weight over time.
  - BMI over time.
  - Weekly completed workouts and calories burned.

### 7. Achievements & Streaks
- **Achievements page**:
  - Grid of unlocked and locked achievements.
  - Criteria and progress per achievement.
- **Streaks**:
  - Current streaks (workout days, on-plan nutrition days).
  - Longest streaks.

### 8. Social & Community
- **Feed / Posts**:
  - Timeline of posts from followed users.
  - Actions: like, comment, create post, view details.
- **My posts**:
  - List and manage own posts (edit/delete).
- **Followers / Following**:
  - List followers and following users.
  - Follow suggestions (mutuals, popular users).

### 9. Challenges
- **Challenges list**:
  - Browse available challenges: type, difficulty, duration, reward.
  - Indicates joined vs not joined.
- **Challenge detail**:
  - Description, rules, duration, reward.
  - Personal progress in challenge.
  - Leaderboard snippet (top participants).

### 10. Notifications
- **Notifications center**:
  - List of notifications grouped by type (workout, challenge, social, system).
  - Actions: open target, mark as read, mark all as read.

### 11. Settings & Account
- **Account settings**:
  - Email (and possibly change password).
  - Language, timezone.
  - Delete/deactivate account.
- **App preferences**:
  - Units (metric/imperial).
  - Default workout and nutrition goals.
  - Notification preferences.

### 12. Admin (for Admin role only)
- **User management**:
  - List/search users.
  - View roles and status.
  - Promote/demote, block/unblock.
- **Content management**:
  - Approve/reject posts and reviews.
  - Manage challenges.
- **Data management**:
  - Manage Food Items and Exercise Library.
  - Basic system stats dashboards.
